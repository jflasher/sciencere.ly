Sciencerely.org
is coming soon!

Sciencerely will be a free platform for individual scientists in any field in any part of the world to find each other to connect, pool resources, and begin collaborating. Scientists - students, postdocs, faculty, researchers - who are interested in forming these collaborations can create individual 'Sciencerely Collaborator' profiles and settings that will automatically let them know when a Collaborator with their specified geographic and scientific interests is open to new collaborations. If a Collaborator finds someone on the site they would like to contact, they can reach out through Sciencerely's site in a way that enables a connection and also respects personal privacy.

Basically, we want to expand and better connect the planet's human brainpower grid to do important (or just cool!) science.
